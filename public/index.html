Welcome to KIIT Mailer

Generate App Password

To send emails securely, you need a Google App Password.
Go to Google Account Settings (ensure you are logged in with your KIIT email).

Create New App Password

In the "App names" field, type "KIIT Mailer" and click Create.

Copy & Save

Copy the 16-character password generated. You will need to enter this in the Settings tab on the next screen.
